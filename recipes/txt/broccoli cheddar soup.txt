Broccoli cheddar soup
Desc:quick broccoli soup
Yield: 2 portions
Type:recipe
Source:https://www.reddit.com/r/ketorecipes/comments/101nbm3/fastest_broccoli_and_cheddar_soup_in_the_west_in/

Ingredients:
broccoli, 480g
chicken broth, 2 cup
heavy cream, 1/4 cup
cheddar, 100g
garlic powder, to taste
pepper, to taste
salt, to taste
onion powder, to taste

Recipe:
Put all ingredients into your blender and blend for about one minute. If you like smoother soup you can blend for longer. pour into bowls and serve.
If your ingredients weren't hot enough you can heat up soup further after you blend.
